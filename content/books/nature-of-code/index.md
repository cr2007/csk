[The Nature of Code](https://natureofcode.com/) is an book by [Daniel Shiffman](https://thecodingtrain.com/) where, in his own words, "explored the unpredictable evolutionary and emergent properties of nature in software".

From what I found looking online, it a way to combine coding with creative projects, and understanding how to better model real-world systems.

I wanted to give this a go to dive into the crossroads of art and technology, and also see how I can build up my problem-solving skills.
